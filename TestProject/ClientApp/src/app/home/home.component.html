TT Club

The table tennis center "TT Club" is the largest specialized table tennis hall in Kharkiv (ping-pong).

How to find: this is a 4-storey "rounded" building 40 meters to the left of the "French Boulevard" shopping center, viewed from Akademik Pavlova, right near the tram station.

The hall is open for visitors daily from 10:00 am to 10:00 pm and is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable game.

Club mission

The mission of the club is the development of our favorite sport in Kharkov and in the country. Our goal is to provide everyone who loves table tennis with the necessary conditions for playing. For those who play for fun and do not set themselves sports tasks, we are given rackets and balls of very good quality.

For corporate clients. The club has experience in specialized sporting events. If you want to further consolidate the team of your company and introduce your employees to the sport, you can enjoy a "private" tournament, with the possibility of renting the entire hall (or part of it) for the time you want. A well-organized sporting event is the best way to improve relations within the team.

For those who want to learn the technique and other intricacies of the game, we have coaches who work both with groups and individually. For children - set in children's groups for training and training. For trained and experienced tennis players - all the conditions for training and a wide choice of partners at various levels.

TT Club activity

Registration for club and rating tournaments - in the "Tournaments" The date of the nearest rating tournament is in the "News" section.

Our employees are experienced tennis players, they love this game, they are friendly and happy to help.
